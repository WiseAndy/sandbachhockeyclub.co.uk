Welcome to the Sandbach Hockey Club

Summer training has now started, we train on Thursday evenings at Sandbach School. If you are new to the club then details of how to join us are below.

Summer Membership

If you are joining us for the first time, fill in a membership form below. You get 3 complimentary trial sessions to with the club before you need to pay for summer membership. Summer membership is £20 and then £3 per session and will run until August 31st.

  • 18+ Trial
  • Under 18 Trial
  • 18+ Summer Member
  • Under 18 Summer Member

Summer Training Times

U1O training - 6pm to 7pm

Junior training - 6:30pm to 7:30pm

Mixed Senior training - 7:30pm to 8:30pm

Please do not enter the pitch early, as you will need to sign in on arrival and wait for any previous session to exit the pitch.
